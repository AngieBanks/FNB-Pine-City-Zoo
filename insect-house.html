INSECT HOUSE

🐛 Insect House – Big Wonders, Tiny Creatures

Dive into the hidden world of the six-legged and many-eyed at the Insect House — a place where the tiny reign supreme. This immersive exhibit takes you on a journey through the lives of some of the planet’s most essential and intriguing creatures.

🦋 What to Explore:
• Live butterfly garden with free-flying beauties
• Glass tunnels revealing the inner workings of ant colonies
• Terrariums featuring beetles, stick insects, spiders, and more
• Fascinating facts on insect defense, camouflage, and communication
• Interactive stations for kids to learn through play

Discover how insects pollinate plants, break down waste, and even inspire technology. With glowing fireflies, powerful beetles, and delicate wings on display, the Insect House will give you a whole new respect for the little things that run the world.

🐜 Come crawl, climb, and buzz your way through a mini-universe of natural wonder.
